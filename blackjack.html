How to play:
Enter a new bet and press New Game. If you press New Game in the middle of the game, you will forfeit your bet.
As long as your hand does not exceed 21 you may press hit for a new card. If you're happy with your hand, you can press Stand
and the dealer will go. The dealer must hit until 17 and doesn't have to hit on a soft 17. You may bet more money than you currently have.
However, you will go into the hole. Excessive amount of time spent in the hole will result in a visit from the mafia.
